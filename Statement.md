A <b>statement</b> is a sentence which is either <b>True</b> (T) or <b>False</b> (F) but both or neither. ^efd55f

Ex. | Tom Brady is a football play => T
Ex. | The Rock is president => F
Ex. | $2 + 2 = 4$ => T
Ex. | $x^2 = 9$ => F
